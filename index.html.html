As people undergo stress everyday, their mental health gets affected.

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being.

Mental Health can affect how we think,act and even how we feel

Some may say "Mental illnesses aren't real" however they are wrong.

Mental illnesses are not things you can touch or directly see on a piece of paper.

One might wonder what are some signs that you may have a mental illness?

Some signs may be:

  • Constant Overthinking

  • Low self esteem

  • Change in sleeping habits

  • Inability to focus

  • Loss of interest in things you enjoy

  • Major increase/decrease in weight

  • Long-lasting sadness

  • withdrawl from social circles

  • Extreme mood changes

  • Apathy
