What is Mental Health?

Mental Health is something that affects our emotional,physical and social well-being. If not dealt with appropiately it may cause the person being affected by it to have suicidal thoughts.

Your Health and emotional well-being should never be ignored!

Mental Health usually stems from:

  • Childhood Abuse

  • Overworking

  • Bullying

  • Having a medical condition

  • Poverty or debt

  • losing someone important to you

  • Long term stress

  • Neglect

  • Trauma

  • Family History
